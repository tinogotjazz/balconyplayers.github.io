With every CD we sell, we sponsor a good project with the profit.

When you purchase our first CD, Balcony Go Crazy,
one tree is planted in India via an organization "Sadhana Forest"
for more information, visit their website:
sadhanaforest.org

And when you purchase our new CD, Balcony Adventures Around the World,
we donate 50cents of the profit to "Balkan Sunflowers."
Balkan Sunflowers is an organization focused on the cultural integration of
Roma and Egyptian tribes in the Kosovo area.
for more information, visit their website:
www.balkansunflowers.org
